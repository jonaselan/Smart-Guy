About the game

Smart Guy is a game developed by students Jonas Elan and Eduardo Marçal. The game was developed in the Ruby language, which was crafted during the entire first quarter in the classroom, with the help of the framework Gosu. The plot tells the story of a boy who wants to get into your apartment, therefore, must collect all the keys scattered in its path. Much of the game has been developed as the sprintes, the scenarios, soundtrack, characters and physics. The following will be shown some of these elements, still under development.

Goals

  • design and implement techniques of computer games;
  • practice the content given in the classroom;
  • complete the last note of the discipline of computer programming;
  • experience of teamwork, which is fundamental in the area in question;
  • Interdisciplinarity between the disciplines of Portuguese and Web Design, respectively, with the construction of a report and website for the game.
